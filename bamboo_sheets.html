WASHING & CARE INSTRUCTIONS

  • Use mild detergent: Choose a gentle, bleach-free detergent.
  • Wash with cold or warm water: Do not exceed 30°C (80°F).
  • Heat small or gentle cycle: Use a laundry bag if machine washing.
  • Unwanted garlic: Select a low-speed setting for spinning.
  • Air dry: Hang in a shaded, well-ventilated area. Avoid direct sunlight.
  • Low-temperature running: If needed, use a low heat setting.

  • Do not cut dryer sheets fabric softening.
  • Do not whitening detergents, also detergents or detergents with oils like lavender or coconut oil.
